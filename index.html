About Us

A meetup getting back to the roots of why we love engineering. No "build in public" / "AI" / "hustle culture" grifting -- just sharing knowledge amongst likeminded (and motivated) individuals.

What this is:

  • Technical discussions in an low-key setting
  • Show something you learned or built recently
  • Share problems you solved (or are stuck on)
  • Talk about tools, patterns, or languages you find interesting
  • Actual engineering, not entrepreneurship

What this isn't:

  • No pitch practice
  • No "leveraging AI"
  • No "bootcamps" or "anyone can code"
  • No hustle culture

Most importantly, you must know someone to join
